# Privacy Policy

**Pocket Verse - The Right Verse for Right Now**

*Last Updated: January 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

Pocket Verse ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Pocket Verse, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Your prompts:** The text you enter describing what's on your heart (e.g., "feeling anxious about work")
- **Saved verses:** Bible verses you choose to save to your collection

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Anonymous user ID:** We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique identifier for your account. This does not require your name, email, or any personal information.
- **Usage data:** Basic app usage statistics such as number of verses generated
- **Subscription status:** Whether you have an active premium subscription

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Your real name
- Your email address
- Your location
- Your contacts
- Device identifiers for advertising

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- **Provide the service:** Your prompts are sent to our AI service to find relevant Bible verses
- **Save your verses:** Store your saved verses so you can access them later
- **Manage subscriptions:** Track your premium status to unlock features
- **Improve the app:** Understand general usage patterns (not individual behavior)

---

## 4. AI Processing

Pocket Verse uses OpenAI's API to analyze your prompts and suggest relevant Bible verses.

- Your prompts are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing
- OpenAI's use of this data is governed by their privacy policy and data processing terms
- We do not use your prompts to train AI models
- Prompts are processed in real-time and are not stored by us beyond what is necessary to deliver the service

---

## 5. Data Storage

- **Saved verses and user data** are stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore)
- **Subscription data** is managed by RevenueCat and Apple/Google
- All data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)

---

## 6. Data Retention

- Your saved verses are retained until you delete them or delete your account
- Anonymous account data is retained as long as you use the app
- If you uninstall the app and do not reinstall within 12 months, your anonymous account may be automatically deleted

---

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

| Third Party | Purpose | Data Shared |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| OpenAI | AI verse matching | Your prompts (text only) |
| Firebase/Google | Data storage & authentication | Anonymous ID, saved verses |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | Anonymous ID, purchase status |
| Apple/Google | Payment processing | Payment info (handled by them) |

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all your saved verses within the app
- **Delete:** Remove individual saved verses or request full account deletion
- **Opt-out:** Stop using the app at any time

To request account deletion, contact us at the email below.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

Pocket Verse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

---

## 10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission (TLS)
- Secure cloud infrastructure (Firebase)
- Anonymous authentication (no passwords to steal)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
